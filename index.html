Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the data practices of the mobile application Player2 TV, developed and operated by Player2Apps.

Important Content & Service Disclaimer

Player2 TV is strictly a media player utility. The application does not provide, host, include, sell, or lease any media content, digital streams, live channels, accounts, access credentials, or playlists. The app is entirely empty upon installation. Users are entirely responsible for providing their own legally acquired content streams or access credentials from their own user-configured servers.

The application does not process payments, process in-app purchases, validate license keys, or provide any subscription services. All functionality is unlocked upon installation.

Data Collection

This app collects the following data solely for core functionality purposes:

  • Android ID – Used to generate a unique device identifier for account management, connection limits, and update checks. This is securely hashed using SHA-256 and cannot be reversed to expose the original ID.
  • Account credentials (username and password) – Stored exclusively locally on your device to authenticate with your user-configured server. These are never transmitted to third parties or our infrastructure.
  • APK version number & hashed device identifier – Sent to the server you configure solely to check for available updates and manage concurrent device connections to your account.

This application does not collect, transmit, or process any data while running in the background or when not actively in use. All data handling occurs entirely during active, foreground user interaction.

Third-Party SDKs and Libraries

This application does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Your data is never shared with, tracked by, or exposed to external marketing or telemetry partners.

Data Storage & Retention

All authentication credentials are stored locally on your device using Android's built-in SharedPreferences infrastructure. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties except the custom server URL you manually input. Hashed device identifiers used for connection limitations are retained only as long as necessary to manage active server sessions or until an explicit deletion request is made.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any personal data or device identifiers with third parties. Your data is used exclusively to facilitate the core functional operations of the application.

Security

All network communication established with your manually configured server utilizes HTTPS encryption protocols where available. Device identifiers are strictly processed as cryptographic hashes and are not retained in plain text.

User Rights & Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and provide full control over your data. Because account credentials are saved entirely locally on your device, you can delete this data instantly at any time by clearing the application cache/data through your Android OS settings or by uninstalling the application. If you wish to request the deletion of any hashed hardware parameters stored on your server platform, please contact your respective server administrator or contact us directly at the email listed below.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any amendments or updates will be published directly to this page.
